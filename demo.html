Research Demo

Natural Language UI for Rare-disease Database.

We build a natural language UI for the NORD rare-disease database. You can search for information about rare-diseases simply by asking questions, such as:
  • What may cause Arthritis, Infectious?
  • What are the possible symptoms of Campomelic Syndrome?
  • If I have a fever, chills, general weakness, and headaches. What disease may I suffer from?
