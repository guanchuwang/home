About me

I am currently a PhD. student in Rice University supervised by Professor Prof. Xia Hu. Before joining Rice University, I was working as a research assistant in Texas A&M University (Prof. Xia Hu group) in 2020 and Westlake university (Professor Donglin Wang group) in 2019. From 2012 to 2019, I recevied B.S. degree from Dalian University of Technology (DUT) and M.S. degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where I was fortunate for having been supervised by Prof. Chen Gong and Zhenyuan Xu.

My research focuses on Efficient ML (especifically for large language model) and Explainable AI (XAI); In addition to my primary focus, I have also undertaken projects in diverse domains such as anomaly detection, deep reinforcement learning, and signal processing. Moreover, I have a keen interest in applying machine learning techniques to address scientific challenges, particularly in the fields of bioinformatics and wireless communication. I will be on the job market in Fall 2024 seeking for academic opportunities.

LLM APP

Natural Language UI for Rare-disease Database.

We build a natural language UI for the NORD rare-disease database. You can search for information about rare-diseases simply by asking questions, such as:
  • What may cause Arthritis, Infectious?
  • What are the possible symptoms of Campomelic Syndrome?
  • If I have a fever, chills, general weakness, and headaches. What disease may I suffer from?
